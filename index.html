Next Beat


Next Beat is my current primary project, and most of my programming effort goes towards its development.
It consists of a Android app portion, which is what the user uses, and a Node.js server which hosts a
RESTful API for the Android app to use.

The point of Next Beat is to be able to play tracks from both Spotify and Soundcloud, seamlessly, without
switching between their respective apps. All playback is controlled through my app, and users are able to
create playlists with tracks from both music sources.

I am currently hosting my app on the app store, in a private beta for my friends to help test it out.
I would love to share the app's source, but since I hope to eventually market and monetize my idea, I
cannot share any of source code. However, I am able to share screenshots from my application, and I hope
these are able to convey my work properly. Please refer to my github, especially the Graphing Calculator
and Schedule Builder applications, which are my most refined programs. Also, my "Library" project is used
throughout many of my programs, and the Node code is very important to some of my software.
